Welcome to Bloom Cafe

Bloom Café is a donation-based, nonprofit third space operated by a wonderful group of people with disabilities.
We operate on a pay what you can model, and aim to be a completely inclusive and accessible space for all.
We serve high quality barista made hot drinks, chai, toasites and baked goods, as well as operate various social groups such as facilitated art therapy and crochet club among others.

Bloom Cafe is open Mondays and Thursdays from 10:00 to 1:00. Come by for great drinks, good food, and an atmosphere that makes you feel at home.

Bloom Café is proud to be a partnership between Frankston Life Community and Living Life Support Services
